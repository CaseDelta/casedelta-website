## Research Methodology

### Sources Used:
1. **Firm Websites** - Attorney bios, contact pages, staff directories
2. **LinkedIn** - Professional profiles, company pages
3. **Google Searches** - Targeted attorney + firm + email searches
4. **Third-Party Databases** - ZoomInfo, RocketReach, Avvo
5. **Bar Association Directories** - Missouri Bar, Kansas Bar, KCMBA
6. **Super Lawyers Profiles** - Peer-verified directories

### Email Patterns Identified:

| Pattern | Example | Prevalence |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| firstname@domain | ron@nelsonyarnell.com | High (solos, small firms) |
| firstname.lastname@domain | thomas.cartmell@wcllp.com | High (large firms) |
| firstname_initial@domain | tcartmell@wcllp.com | Medium |
| first_last@domain | jb@bflawfirm.com | Medium |
| firstnamelastinitial@domain | timb@firm.com | Low |

### Domain Patterns:
- Small/solo: firstname.com, lastnamelawfirm.com
- Large firms: Standard firm domain (wcllp.com, lathropgpm.com)
- Email formats depend on firm size/structure

## Key Findings

### Success Factors:
1. **Super Lawyers/Board Leaders** = High success rate (93% found)
   - More prominent online presence
   - Better website optimization
   - More direct contact information

2. **Large Firm Attorneys** = Moderate success (50-70% if directly sought)
   - Firm websites have staff directories
   - Standard email format patterns
   - Some firms limit public email exposure

3. **Solo Practitioners** = Low success (10-20%)
   - Minimal web presence
   - Limited public email listings
   - Often require phone inquiry

### Recommendations for Remaining 40:

**Priority Tier 1 (Higher confidence):**
- Contact large firms (Wagstaff, Spencer Fane, Davis Bethune, Dollar Burns) directly

**Priority Tier 3 (Lower confidence):**
